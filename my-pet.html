Sam's cat, Remus

Remus is a two-year-old domestic shorthair. I adopted him from a shelter when he was a kitten.

He has tabby coloring with some white and black marks as well.

Some of his favorite things include:

  • Waking up his owner in the morning with head taps to remind her to give him his breakfast
  • Running around like a crazy person at 2am
  • Playing with his favorite blue feather fishing toy
  • Sleeping on top of his cat tree

Rochelle's dog, Pippa

Pippa is a two-year-old Havanese dog. She has white hair and a small nose.

Some of her favorite things include:

  • Going on long walks
  • Doing tricks for treats
  • Playing with her zebra toy
  • Sleeping on the couch
